Outbreak at the Clinic

A crisis has emerged at the interdimensional clinic. Clinicians must identify and quarantine the saboteurs before they neutralize everyone.

Operational Protocols

Overview

Future Hooman: Outbreak at the Clinic is a social deduction game where staff are secretly assigned roles. Clinicians must work together to identify and quarantine the Inoculator before everyone is neutralized.

Factions

  • Clinicians: PHAX staff and aligned operators who want to quarantine the Inoculator
  • SigSev: Signal Severed saboteurs who want to neutralize Clinicians

Game Flow

  1. Setup: Helper Bot 3000 assigns secret roles to all staff
  2. Power Cycle (Stasis): Staff with stasis actions perform their abilities
  3. Between Power Cycles: Staff discuss, share information, and vote on who to quarantine
  4. Repeat: Continue alternating between stasis and active cycles until the outbreak resolves

Victory Conditions

  • Clinicians win if the Inoculator is quarantined
  • SigSev wins if only 2 staff remain (1 Clinician, 1 SigSev)
