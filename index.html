Blood on the Clocktower

FuHoo Outbreak Edition

A social deduction game where good fights evil. The town must find and execute the demon before it kills them all.

Game Rules

Overview

Blood on the Clocktower is a bluffing game where players are secretly assigned roles. Good players must work together to identify and execute the Demon before they're eliminated.

Teams

  • Good Team: Townsfolk and Outsiders who want to execute the Demon
  • Evil Team: Minions and the Demon who want to eliminate good players

Game Flow

  1. Setup: The Storyteller assigns secret roles to all players
  2. Night Phase: Players with night actions wake up and perform their abilities
  3. Day Phase: Players discuss, share information, and vote on who to execute
  4. Repeat: Continue alternating between night and day until the game ends

Winning

  • Good wins if the Demon is executed
  • Evil wins if only 2 players remain alive (1 good, 1 evil)
